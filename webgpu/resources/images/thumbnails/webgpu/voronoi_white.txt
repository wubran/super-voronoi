This is a precursor of this website.
It had no image textures, minimal mouse interaction, and a simple kernel-based edge detection method.
The straight lines of the cell boundaries indicate that I was using a different distance metric to calculate cell boundaries.
I deemed the edge drawing too expensive for less-fortunate devices, and cut it out.

This site uses your GPU to render a power diagram.
Specifically, this site renders the intersection of a 3D weighted Voronoi Diagram with a flat plane.
By scrolling back and forth, you control the height of this plane.
