I have been 3D-Printing since early middle school. Maybe 2016?
My first printer was originally intended to be my older brother's, but I ended up taking it up instead.
It was awful. A cheap, horrifically unreliable Monoprice cantilever. No amount of tuning could have saved it from the scrap bin.
It taught me practical skills about how 3D printers work... skills that many people entering the hobby today lack.
I have nothing against Bambu though. They saved me a deadline once or twice.
My current printer is a Creality CR-10 with dampeners on the steppers.
